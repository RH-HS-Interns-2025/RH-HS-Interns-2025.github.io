First Day @ Red Hat!

A picture of me out front of the Red Hat building! It was my first day at Red Hat for the High School Internship Program. I didn't really know what to expect but I had an amazing first day. I was amazed at how relaxed the environment was and the free snacks that were available!

King's Dominion

On April 26th, 2025, I went to King's Dominion with the Franklinton High School's Choral Program to compete in the Music in the Parks competition. While being able to go to King's Dominion was a huge incentive for a lot of us, we still put in the effort to learn and polish the music we planned on singing for the judges.

And our hardwork paid off! We were able to score 2nd place in our category and brought back a trophy! I enjoyed riding roller coasters with my friends and peers, making memories I won't ever forget.

Duke Math Meet

During my sophomore year of high school, I was given the opportunity to compete at Duke University's Math Meet. Our school's team didn't expect to win anything, we just wanted to have fun and eat a good lunch. As long as we got 1 point, we'd be satisifed. It was an enjoyable experience to go with my friends as well as take memorable pictures like this one. Though, I would have to critique that the food wasn't that good and they ran out of it quickly.

Hobby or Addiction?

Sometimes, I don't know what the line between a hobby or an addiction is. Which... sounds really bad. As an avid reader, I really love reading books and as such, I buy a lot of them and sometimes when I buy them, I don't even read them. All the time, people would tell me to just borrow books but that doesn't really seem ideal to me. I do eventually get to reading the books I buy but seeing how big my to be read list is, I might be set until 2026!

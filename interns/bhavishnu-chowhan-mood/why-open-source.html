Our Core Business Model

Why Open Source?

RedHat believes in making technology more accessible for all people. By using an open source model, we share our software models publicly for all to access. This contributes to a more collaborative ecosystem in the tech space. Additionally, we stay at the forefront of innovation by incorporating diverse perspectives and ideas from the community.

Customer Priority

At RedHat, our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We prioritize their needs and feedback, ensuring that our open-source solutions are not only innovative but also practical and user-friendly. With an open source model, our work is easily accessible to our customers, allowing for authentic feedback throughout the design process.

What You'll Find

Flexibility

Software can be modified to your business-specific needs. According to your model, we can adapt to various workflows and gain a unique advantage in the market.

Transparency

We believe in being open about our processes, decisions, and the challenges we face. This transparency fosters trust and collaboration, both within our team and with our customers.

Community

Our employees are empowered to explore new ideas, take risks, and push boundaries without the constraints of traditional corporate structures.

Stay Ahead.

RedHat is committed to continuous improvement and innovation. By leveraging the power of open source, we can quickly adapt to changing market demands and stay ahead of the competition. Our collaborative approach allows us to harness the collective expertise of our community, ensuring that we are always at the forefront of technological advancements.

Day 3

What are some of the differences you've noticed between a school environment and a professional work environment?

Some major differences I've found between a school environment and a professional work environment is the difference in freedom, responsibility, and ability to do things. I think school is great to increase your knowledge, but I think that a professional work environment helps a ton with experience of the real world, and applications of what we've learned in school in the real world. School environments are more learning oriented, and you learn a ton in professional work environments, but you also apply that in the real world.

Thinking of all of the possible issues the consumer is having, thinking of what to ask, and then what to implement to fix that issue, is a lot harder than it might seem.

Day 8

How has this experience affected or changed your career/college plans?

This experience has definitely enhanced and acted as a motivator in terms of my college plans. Its made me realize what sectors I'm interested in, and has provided valuable insights and information to those sectors. I’ve talked with people, heard their stories from the beginning of college to now, and after speaking to them, I actually feel a bit less stressed. One commonality I’ve found with almost every person I’ve spoken to here at Red Hat, is they all said you don’t always immediately know what you’re gonna want to do. The job market and innovation is everchanging, and the most important skill to help you thrive in it, is to change with it. This experience has enhanced and encouraged my desire for a college degree, and a focus on my future.
